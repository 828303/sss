KingSomNala Tehillah Songs is a brainchild of KingSomNala the founder, first citizen of the world of plenty, preacher, psalmist, senior pastor of Rebirth Family Church International and the King of the plentian nation. It was conceived from a deep passion of KingSomNala to use music as a vehicle to carry the deepest expression of the human heart to god and to equip,teach people with the ability to make music an instrument of praising and worshipping god. It was born in the city of Durban as a worship team in 1988 and has recorded 6 albums. It objectve is to improve the standard of music in our contry by offering music skills,in terms of singing,writing new songs, how to behave as a musician or how to handle the spot light,teachings on music as a carrer and business,discovering different artistic potential and naturing them to be all rounded artists as we also do stage plays and dance.
